Steps Every Smart Buyer Takes Before Choosing a Home in Yelahanka

Buying a home is one of life's biggest milestones. But with excitement comes responsibility. Smart buyers don't just look at glossy brochures; they dig deeper into the data, the location, and the future potential.

BEGIN YOUR JOURNEY
Your Checklist
VERIFY RERA REGISTRATION

Start with the basics. Ensure the project is registered under RERA. This Act ensures transparency, accountability, and grievance redressal. It is your first and most important safeguard against delays and defaults.

YOUR FIRST AND MOST IMPORTANT SAFEGUARD
KNOW YOUR TRUE CARPET AREA

Don't be misled by "Super Built-up Area" or "Saleable Area." Focus on the Carpet Area, the actual net usable space inside your walls. This is the space you live in, and this is what truly determines the value of your purchase.

THE SPACE YOU LIVE IN DETERMINES TRUE VALUE
ANALYZE THE GROWTH TRAJECTORY (THE 25.3%* RULE)

Smart buyers don't just buy a home; they buy an asset. Look at the historical data. Yelahanka has quietly transformed, recording a massive 25.3%* growth in real estate value recently. While other markets saturate, Yelahanka is entering its "Second Wave" of appreciation, fueled by infrastructure maturity.

25.3%* GROWTH | SECOND WAVE OF APPRECIATION
CHECK THE "LUNG POWER" (AQI ADVANTAGE)

In a city struggling with pollution, the most luxurious amenity is fresh air. Unlike the congested CBD, Yelahanka benefits from a massive protected green zone (Defense land & GKVK). This keeps the AQI consistently healthier and the micro-climate often 2°C cooler than central Bangalore. Ensure your future home is within this "Green Shield."

2°C COOLER | GREEN SHIELD ADVANTAGE
DEFINE YOUR "15-MINUTE ECOSYSTEM"

Smart buyers look at what lies outside the gates as much as what is inside. In Yelahanka, you don't need to travel hours for essentials. Check if your project connects to these hubs:

Education: NITTE Meenakshi, BMS Institute, DPS, Ryan International.
Healthcare: Aster CMI, Manipal Hospital, Cytecare Cancer Hospital.
Lifestyle: Mall of Asia, RMZ Galleria, Bhartiya Mall of Bengaluru.
Work Hubs: Manyata Tech Park, L&T Tech Park, and the upcoming Airport City.
Connectivity: The 15-minute signal-free run to KIAL and the upcoming Blue Line Metro.

15-MINUTE SIGNAL-FREE RUN TO KEMPEGOWDA INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT
DECIDE ON CONSTRUCTION STAGE (RISK VS. REWARD)

Weigh the pros and cons of Under-Construction vs. Ready-to-Move:

Cost: Under-construction homes are often priced lower, offering an immediate paper profit as the project nears completion.
Tax: GST (12%) applies to under-construction units, but they often yield higher ROI.
Quality Check: Buying early allows you to monitor the construction quality and materials (like Mivan technology) from the ground up.

EARLY BUYERS CAN MONITOR QUALITY
LOOK FOR "NEXT-GEN" GREENERY

Don't settle for basic landscaping. Modern wellness homes are designed around curated, purpose-led green spaces - such as therapeutic walking trails, shaded groves, and biodiversity-focused landscapes. Projects with thoughtful, IGBC-aligned landscape planning help lower ambient temperatures and support better mental well-being for your family.

IGBC-ALIGNED FOR MENTAL WELL-BEING
EVALUATE THE DEVELOPER'S TRACK RECORD

History repeats itself. Study the developer's past projects. Did they deliver on time? How is the maintenance years later? Financial stability and a strong portfolio are the best indicators that your home will be delivered as promised.

GODREJ: 127+ YEARS OF TRUST
ASSESS THE AMENITIES THAT MATTER

Look beyond the standard swimming pool. Does the project have a fully equipped clubhouse, co-working spaces for the hybrid era, and dedicated sports zones? In a self-sustaining hub like Yelahanka, your community amenities become your daily lifestyle.

AMENITIES BECOME YOUR DAILY LIFESTYLE
KEEP YOUR DOCUMENTS & FINANCES READY

Before you book, ensure your financial health is in order. Keep your PAN card, Aadhaar, and a cancelled cheque ready. Remember, tax benefits under Section 24 and 80C only apply if possession is taken within the stipulated time, so plan your finances with a clear budget in mind.

TAX BENEFITS UNDER SECTION 24 & 80C
